About Us

We turn meals into stories. Every bite celebrates our passion for food, blending tradition with modern tastes.

Brand Story

CAVAZA’s story began in 2016 in Dehradun, where Nishant’s passion for flavours sparked a culinary revolution. Driven by creativity and innovation, CAVAZA grew into a brand that transforms food into unforgettable experiences. Now rooted in Delhi NCR, we carry forward the same spirit—crafting dishes that blend tradition with modern tastes, delivering unmatched quality, and celebrating the joy of good food every day.

Our Mission

To craft culinary masterpieces that blend tradition with innovation, delighting taste buds and uplifting spirits with every dish, while delivering unmatched quality and a memorable dining experience.

Our Vision

To be a leading name in redefining culinary excellence, inspiring joy and connection through food, and setting new standards for innovation and quality in the global food industry.

What Sets Us Apart

At CAVAZA, we redefine the dining experience by combining passion, innovation, and the finest ingredients.

Uncompromising Food Safety Standards

Strict hygiene and food safety protocols, ensuring every dish is prepared in a safe and clean environment.

Nutritious, Home-Quality Ingredients

Fresh and premium ingredients, ensure that every meal is as wholesome and nutritious as home-cooked food.

Innovative Culinary Creations

Traditional flavours meet modern culinary twists, delighting every palate.

Versatile Offerings

From snacks to meals. we cater to all occasions with diverse, flavourful options.

Nishant Gulsia - Founder

Welcome to our culinary journey! Born and raised in the vibrant streets of Delhi, with deep roots in Punjab, my passion for food has always been a guiding force in my life. Growing up, I was enchanted by the rich Mughlai flavors that danced on my palate, while the diverse tastes of Bengali, Rajasthani, and Marwadi cuisines fueled my adventurous spirit. My love for Italian and American dishes adds a global twist to my culinary repertoire.

For nearly two decades, I have thrived in a corporate role within the People Development domain, where I focus on nurturing talent and fostering growth. Simultaneously, I have dedicated almost a decade to the food business, blending my professional expertise with my culinary passion. I believe in the transformative power of food—not just as nourishment for the body, but as a source of joy and connection for the mind. My mission is to positively impact the lives of those around me through the art of cooking and sharing knowledge.

Join me as we explore the world of flavors together, celebrating the happiness that good food brings!
